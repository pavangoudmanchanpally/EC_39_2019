\section{Reduction of logical circuit}
The output of 2:1 mux is P.\\
Now , $P=AQ+A'Q'$\\
$D=(Q.P)'$\\
$D=(Q(AQ+A'Q'))'$\\
 $D=(A(Q.Q)+(A'Q'Q))'$
 $D=(AQ)'$\\
 The equation after reducing the logical circuit is:\\
 $D=(AQ)'$\\
